# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** December 9, 2025

## Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of Genesis and is designed to protect our users, platform, and community.

## Prohibited Content

You may **NOT** create, upload, or distribute content that:

### Illegal Activities
- Violates any local, national, or international law
- Promotes or facilitates illegal activities
- Infringes on intellectual property rights
- Contains pirated or stolen content

### Harmful Content
- Promotes violence, self-harm, or dangerous activities
- Contains hate speech or discrimination based on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality
- Harasses, threatens, or bullies individuals
- Exploits or endangers minors

### Inappropriate Content
- Contains pornography or sexually explicit material
- Depicts graphic violence or gore
- Promotes substance abuse or illegal drugs
- Glorifies criminal activities

### Deceptive Practices
- Impersonates individuals or organizations
- Spreads misinformation or disinformation
- Contains phishing attempts or scams
- Misrepresents affiliation with Genesis

## Prohibited Activities

You may **NOT**:

### System Abuse
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems
- Upload viruses, malware, or malicious code
- Interfere with the Service's operation
- Reverse engineer or decompile our software
- Use automated bots or scrapers without permission

### Account Abuse
- Create multiple free accounts to bypass limits
- Share accounts with others
- Sell or transfer your account
- Use another person's account without permission

### Resource Abuse
- Generate excessive API requests
- Create content solely to consume resources
- Exploit bugs or vulnerabilities
- Bypass tier limitations or restrictions

### Spam and Unwanted Content
- Send unsolicited messages or invitations
- Post repetitive or irrelevant content
- Engage in coordinated inauthentic behavior
- Manipulate gamification systems

## Commercial Use Restrictions

### Free Tier (Spark)
- Content is watermarked
- **NO** commercial use permitted
- Personal and educational use only

### Paid Tiers (Creator+)
- Commercial use permitted
- Must comply with all other policies
- Watermarks removed

## Intellectual Property

### Your Responsibilities
- Ensure you have rights to all content you upload
- Respect copyrights, trademarks, and patents
- Only use images and text you're authorized to use
- Attribute sources where required

### AI-Generated Content
- AI-generated content may inadvertently resemble existing works
- You are responsible for reviewing and modifying AI outputs
- Do not claim AI-generated content as human-created where disclosure is required

## Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that violates this AUP:

**Email**: abuse@genesis.ai  
**In-App**: Use the "Report" button  
**Response Time**: We review reports within 48 hours

## Enforcement

### Warning System
1. **First Violation**: Warning email and content removal
2. **Second Violation**: Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
3. **Third Violation**: Permanent account termination

### Immediate Termination
We reserve the right to immediately terminate accounts that:
- Post illegal content
- Engage in severe harassment or threats
- Compromise platform security
- Create child exploitation material

### Appeals Process
If you believe your account was suspended in error:
1. Email **appeals@genesis.ai** within 14 days
2. Provide detailed explanation and evidence
3. We will review and respond within 7 business days

## Legal Cooperation

We cooperate with law enforcement and may:
- Report illegal activities to authorities
- Preserve evidence for investigations
- Comply with valid legal requests
- Testify in legal proceedings if required

## Educational Use

### Permitted
- Schools and educational institutions may use Genesis for teaching
- Students may create content for assignments
- Teachers may share student work with permission

### Restrictions
- Must comply with COPPA for users under 13
- Parental consent required for minors
- Educational pricing available (contact sales@genesis.ai)

## API and Developer Use

If you use our API or developer features:
- Follow rate limits and quotas
- Attribute Genesis in your application
- Do not create competing services
- Comply with API Terms of Service

## White-Label and Studio Users

If you use white-label or team features:
- You are responsible for your team members' actions
- Monitor and moderate team-created content
- Implement your own content policies
- Ensure team members accept these terms

## Updates to This Policy

We may update this AUP as needed. Material changes will be communicated via:
- Email notification
- Platform announcement
- Updated "Effective Date"

Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.
